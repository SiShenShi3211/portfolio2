How I take on projects like these



Web development process


  • I start off by making an outline, I hear what you need, and then plan out the order of pages alongside their purposes.
  • I make a sketch, usually on Figma. I make sure to take into account the style you are going for. When I am done with the sketch I will get confirmation from you!
  • This is the part where I start coding! I use the sketch as a template until the product is finalizd. Testing is done after to make sure it is responsive and to your liking!
  • After everything is finalized, I can either give you the files or help you set up a server.

    • Unity game process


  • I am familiar with most aspects of unity, the process would vary depending on what your needs are.
  • In most cases, I create basic models for testing code, then move on to develop more complex models and scenes after.

    • 3D modeling process


  • First, I start off with any sketch you give me. If requested I also look for reference material online for assistance.
  • I create an inital model, after it is approved I move on to texturing
  • After the texturing and modeling is completed I can send you the files in any format.
